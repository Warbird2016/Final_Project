Research Paper review and citation
With the number of social network users expected to increase to 2.44 billion by 2018, many companies have begun to measure the impact of social media advertisements. This paper uses predictive systems to predict the evolution of a post prior to its publication. Twelve FaceBook KPI's were selected to predict how posts would evolve, giving managers new insights into managerial decisions to improve brand recognition. Citation: S.Moro, et. al. "Predicting social media performance metrics and evaluation of the impact on brand building: A data mining approach", Journal of Business Research, 2016
